How to Book a Party via Jollibee Kids Party Website


A. Reserve a party as Member

1. Login to Jollibee Kids Party website

  • Head over to the Jollibee Kids Party booking site using your browser. Locate and click the “Login” button to proceed to your account.

2. Access your profile

  • Enter your email address and password, then click “Sign In”. Locate and click the “My Account” button.

3. Go Back to Homepage

  • Click the Jollibee Kids Partylogo and you will be redirected to the"Homepage".

4. Search and select City

  • Input the City name then select or choose theCity from the drop down.

5. Choose Date of Party

  • Select from the calendar your preferredDate of the Party
  • click the “Book a Party” button.
NOTE: You can only book a party 5 days in advance.

6. Enter Party Details

  • Choose your preferred Branch from the dropdown.
  • Input your Number of Guests (minimum of 30).
  • Select Party Zone sure to check room Capacity for each party zone.

7. Select and Enter Party Package

    Choose your Party Theme:
  • Jollitown
  • Fairytale Land
  • Jollirace

    • • Choose Party Meals (Meal Bundle or Create Your Own).

    • If choosing a Meal Bundle:
    Select from Bundles A, B, C, or D and enter the order count (must match the minimum number of guests).

8. Enter Customer and Celebrant's Information

  • First Name
  • Last Name
  • Country Code
  • Mobile Number
  • Email Address
  • Relationship to the Celebrant
  • Notes or Remarks (Optional)

Celebrant's Information:

  • First Name
  • Last Name
  • Party Banner Nickname / Greeting
  • Birthdate (Day, Month, Year)
  • Type of Celebration
You may click +Add Another Celebrant to register multiple celebrants.
  • Tick the consent box for processing your personal information.
  • Tick the consent box for processing your personal information.
  • Click “Reserve Now and Pay Later”.

9. Successful Booking Confirmation

  • After completing your reservation, you will be redirected to the booking successful confirmation page.
- Expect a confirmation email within 3 hours, which includes:
  • Booking successful confirmation
  • Once reservation is completed, you will be redirected to the booking successful confirmation page.
  • Please also expect a booking confirmation via the email address entered during the booking process within 3 hours upon completion of reservation.
  • The booking confirmation email will include the booking ID and details of the party with Terms and Conditions PDF file attachment.
