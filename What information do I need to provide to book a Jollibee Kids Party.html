What information do I need to provide to book a Jollibee Kids Party

When booking a party, customer information and celebration information needs to be provided.

1. Customer information

  • First Name
  • Last Name
  • Country Code
  • Mobile Number
  • Email Address
  • Relationship to the Celebrant
  • Notes or Remarks (Optional)

2. Celebrant information

  • First Name
  • Last Name
  • Party Banner Nickname / Greeting
  • Birthdate Day, Month, Year
  • Type of Celebration
