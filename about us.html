About Jollibee

We deliver flavor to your doorstep, creating memorable meals with every order.

More than 45 years of sharing the joy!

Jollibee is the largest fast food chain brand in the Philippines, operating a network of more than 1,600 stores in 17 countries.

A dominant market leader in the Philippines, Jollibee enjoys the lion's share of the local market that is more than all the other multinational fast food brands in PH combined. With a strict adherence to the highest standards of food quality, service and cleanliness, Jollibee serves great-tasting, high-quality and affordable food products including Chickenjoy, Yumburger, and Jolly Spaghetti.

Jollibee continues to expand internationally, with more than 270 branches across the United States, Canada, China, Brunei, Vietnam, Singapore, Malaysia, Saudi Arabia, UAE, Qatar, Oman, Kuwait, Bahrain, Italy, Spain, and the United Kingdom.
