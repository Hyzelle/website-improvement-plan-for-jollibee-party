Look for the Jollibee Kids Party website or social media pages for information about venue capacity or contact details for inquiries or you may follow below steps:



  • Head over to Jollibee Kids Party booking site using your browser.
  • Input the “City” name then select or choose the “City” from the drop-down.
  • Select from the calendar your preferred “Date of party”.
  • Click “Book a Party” button.

NOTE: You can only book a party 5 days in advance.
