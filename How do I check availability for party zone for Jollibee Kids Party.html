How do I check availability for party zone for Jollibee Kids Party

  • Head over to JJollibee Kids Party booking site using your browser.
  • Input the "City" name then select or choose the "City" from the drop-down.
  • Select from the calendar your preferred "Date of party".
NOTE: You can only book a party 5 days in advance.
  • Click "Book a Party" button.
  • Choose your preferred Branch from the drop-down.
  • Input your Number of Guest (minimum *30*).
  • Select Party Zone and make sure to check room *Capacity* for each party zone.
  • Select an Available Timeslot, then click Next button.
NOTE: Booking confirmation will be sent once the booking is successful.
