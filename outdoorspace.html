Is there an outdoor area or space available for Jollibee Kids Party

6 months ago


Jollibee Kids Party is known for its fully decorated, themed party areas inside Jollibee restaurants, offering interactive games, and appearances by Jollibee and friends. Since the availability of outdoor spaces can vary by location, it's essential to contact the Jollibee branch directly to inquire about their specific options and policies regarding outdoor events. Please indicate details of the activities upon booking in the Step 2-Enter Details under Notes or Remarks field. You can check the Jollibee Kids Party website for the contact details of your preferred Branch by following below steps.



  • Head over to Jollibee Kids Party booking site using your browser.
  • Input the “City” name then select or choose the “City” from the drop-down.
  • Select from the calendar your preferred “Date of party”.
  • Click “Book a Party” button.

NOTE: You can only book a party 5 days in advance.


  • Choose your preferred Branch from the drop-down.
  • You should be able to see Store Information i.e. Address and Contact Number.
