• A Jollibee Fairytale Land party is a themed birthday celebration for children that transforms the party area into a magical, princess-like world. The theme includes decorations like castles and fairy tale graphics, and it can be customized with a magical video invitation to set the tone for a whimsical adventure filled with joy and excitement.


  • A Jollibee JolliRace party is a race-car-themed birthday celebration, available as both an in-person and virtual event. The party features a race car theme and includes elements like games and a mascot appearance for a fun and energetic experience. It's designed for all ages, providing a festive atmosphere for a birthday party.


  • The Jollitown Party is a popular theme offered in the Jollibee Kids Party package, transforming the venue into the festive, fictional world of Jollibee and his friends. It is designed to provide a fun and memorable celebration for children, featuring themed decorations, games, and the potential for a mascot appearance.
